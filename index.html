Dive Cebu

Hi! At Dive Cebu we love diving and provide full diving tours at different locations for both novice and experienced divers

Love Diving!

From diving with the Whale Sharks at Oslob to the marine sanctuary at Naluasan we offer Snorkelling and Scuba Diving for the novice and the experienced diver

We are also passionate about maritime preservation we offer education to people of all ages about how we as Human Beings can help protect the Marine Environment

Dive Locations

Hiluntungan

The dive site of Hiluntungan is well know for it's large schools of fish and is an excellent site ffor divers of all experience levels

Explore More
Nalusuan

Nalusuan is a marine sanctuary, so it is protected from local fisherman, and starts shallow which makes for some beautiful snorkelling and lots of species can be seen such as snappers, angelfish, triggerfish, groupers and all the natural reef fishes shoaling around the different species of corals. Stingrays, trurtles and barracudas can also be seen on a regular basis.

Explore More
Olango

The Santa Rosa dive site at Olango features a steep slope with depths of up to 50m making it easy to visit marine life at different depths in a short period of time.

Explore More
Oslob - Whale Sharks

At Oslob it is possible to snorkel or dive with the massive and beautiful Whale Sharks. These harmless creatures swim gracefully along the surface scooping up plangtum.
